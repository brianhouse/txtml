DESCRIPTION

<i>format</i> simply formats the contained string.


PARAMETERS

<i>format</i> (required) - the desired format
<i>value</i> (optional) - the string to be formatted


EXAMPLES

[...	
	{format format="uppercase" value="a string" /}
...]
[...	
	{format format="uppercase"}
		a string
	{/format}
...]
